The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) is the official youth council in every barangay in the Philippines, designed to represent the interests and voice of the Filipino youth. Through SK, young people participate in governance, organize community programs, and promote youth development initiatives. Guided by elected youth leaders, the SK empowers young citizens to lead in areas like education, health, environment, and sports. With SK, the youth are given a platform to influence local decision-making and help build stronger, more inclusive communities across the country.
Our mission as the Sangguniang Kabataan is to uplift and empower the Filipino youth through meaningful leadership, inclusive programs, and active participation in community development. We aim to be the voice of the youth in local governance, fostering values of integrity, service, and innovation to help build a better and more united society.
Our vision is a generation of empowered, responsible, and proactive Filipino youth — united in purpose, equipped to lead, and dedicated to driving positive change in their communities. Through the Sangguniang Kabataan, we envision a future where young people are at the forefront of nation-building, shaping a just, inclusive, and progressive society.
